About

Sounds without limits

Punchy Punchy Records is an independent label created to push music with identity, energy and production vision.

We work with artists, producers and projects moving between urban, electronic, alternative and organic sounds: from trap, house, drum and bass and electropop to funk, folk, downtempo and hard-to-label hybrids.

The idea is simple: authentic, powerful music with a future-facing vision.

Demos

Got music to show?

If you are working on an urban, electronic, alternative or hard-to-label project, you can submit your demo for us to listen.

We accept demos, finished songs, ideas in development and projects with their own identity.

Please send listening links. Do not attach heavy files.
